Scientific acknowledgments

All of Us and CCDG reference panel

We gratefully acknowledge participants from the National Institutes of Health’s All of Us Research Program and the Centers for Common Disease Genomics (CCDG), without whom this imputation resource would not have been possible. We also thank the All of Us and CCDG grantees for collaborating with the Broad Institute to produce this reference panel.

We ask that users of this service acknowledge the contribution of those who take part in All of Us and CCDG when presenting and publishing their research. Researchers using this imputation service can use the citations here to reference All of Us.

We thank Brian Browning (University of Washington), Franjo Ivankovic (Broad Institute), and Arthur Ko (NIH/NHGRI) for their contributions to preparing the reference panel.
